Common questions

Everything you'd ask a friend.

Compounded semaglutide uses the same active pharmaceutical ingredient (semaglutide) as the branded versions. Our 503B pharmacy partners source USP-grade API. Compounded medication is not FDA-approved; its use is permitted under FDA policies when commercial versions are in shortage or clinically unsuitable for a specific patient.
Sculpted is not appropriate for people with a personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma, Multiple Endocrine Neoplasia syndrome type 2, or a history of pancreatitis. It is also not appropriate during pregnancy, nursing, or when trying to conceive. Your clinician will review your full history before prescribing.
The most common side effects are gastrointestinal: nausea, constipation, and, less often, vomiting. These are typically mild and transient, especially with the slow titration protocol our clinicians use. Most members find that symptoms resolve within the first 4–6 weeks. Your care team is available 24/7 to support any concerns.
